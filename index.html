About SNM Spices

Rooted in a Legacy
SNM Spices was born from a simple flour mill started in 2008 in Kerala. Our founder had one goal: to make pure, healthy food accessible to every family. What began as a small dream has grown into a movement — for honesty, purity, and tradition in everything we touch.

Today, the founder's three sons support the journey:

  • Son 1: A naturopathy doctor with international healing experience.
  • Son 2: A creative mechanic and world traveler passionate about food and spices.
  • Son 3: Works closely with farmers and manages sourcing, logistics, and business development.

Together, they continue SNM’s mission to promote clean, honest food and wellness around the world.

Our Journey

  • 2008: Started with a local flour mill in Kerala
  • 2015: Introduced small-batch spice blends
  • 2019: Shifted to 100% natural sourcing
  • 2023: Launched SNM Spices brand
  • 2025: Website launch & Kerala delivery
  • 2028: Plan to offer lifestyle recipes and health coaching
  • 2035: Vision to create SNM Wellness Center in India
