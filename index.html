Our Story

At SNM Spices, we don't just pack powders — we preserve purity.

What started as a humble flour mill in 2008 in Vilayur village, Kerala, has grown into a mission to deliver truly chemical-free, unadulterated spices to families who care about their health.

Our founding belief is simple: “If we feed our families with love, we must feed them with purity too.”

SNM Spices is a family-run initiative rooted in tradition and health.

Our father started a small flour mill in 2008 — not to grow a big business, but to ensure the people in our village had access to clean, hygienic, and pure grains.

Over time, his concern shifted to spices — often mixed with chemicals, colorants, and harmful preservatives in the market. So he began grinding spices himself — one batch at a time — to protect our plates and our people.

Years later, his son — a naturopathy doctor with international experience — returned home, shocked to learn how even certified products contained slow poisons. Another son, a mechanic and video editor, returned from Dubai to explore India’s spice varieties and reconnect with roots. The youngest supported with sourcing and farmer connections.

Together, we created SNM Spices — a brand built on truth, trust, and health.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To bring back trust in food — starting with the spices we use every day.

We are doctor-led, farmer-connected, and village-rooted. We support organic farming, local health, and ethical living.

We believe healing begins with the basics — and food is medicine.

Our Journey

  • 2008 – Flour mill begins in Vilayur
  • 2016 – Family starts focusing on spice purity
  • 2023 – SNM Spices officially launched
  • 2025 – Production and local supply begins
  • 2026 – Launch of Beena’s Kitchen (healthy lifestyle recipes)
  • 2027 – First physical outlet of Beena’s Kitchen
  • 2028–2029 – Reach every home in Vilayur village
  • 2030–2035 – Open wellness center for lifestyle change support
